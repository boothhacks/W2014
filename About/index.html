Learn how to code with Booth Hacks! An exciting 8-week course in the winter quarter!

Ever wanted to learn how to code but didn't know how to get started? Booth Hacks is launching an 8-week course over winter quarter to teach the basics on coding to all who are interested! Each week Booth Hacks will host a 3 hour session (1 hour of lecture and 2 hours of practice) covering topics from a syllabus. This will all cumulate in week 8 with a hackathon, the winner of which will get great prizes, glory, and the coveted title of "Booth's Best Hacker". For more details an information please join our facebook group.
